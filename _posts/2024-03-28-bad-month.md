A series of bad event happened to me this month. In my early working days, I learnt to take responsibility first. By default, I’ll question my own actions before pointing finger. I’ll blame myself first. There must be something I can do to avoid any bad outcome. Then I learnt from it.

This time is different. So different that I chose to blame the month. Because of bad month then I got these bad events.

Sometimes it is ok to point finger. It’s ok.
