Uses

Sharing the hardware and software I use in my day-to-day life.

Study room

  • MacBook Air M1 - I'm happy with it, may consider 15" model in the next upgrade.
  • Apple Thunderbolt Display - Bought it cheap ($220) at Carousell. The best 2K display. Will change to Apple Studio Display when I'm feeling rich.
  • IKEA SIGFINN Monitor Stand - I needed my monitor to be higher that my MacBook Air, was using stack of books previously.
  • Razer Mouse & Mouse Pad - Don't ask me the model, I've been using it for years! All I know is the old reliable wired model.
  • IKEA VADHOLMA Kitchen Island - Yeah, I use it as my work desk. A bit overkill if you ask me now. But I can seat or stand while working without any adjustment.
  • IKEA FRANKLIN Bar Stool - Not the most comfortable, but it works.

Living room

  • Sony 40" LCD TV - This trusty tv doesn’t give me an excuse to replace it!
  • Apple TV HD - To make my tv smarter.
  • Apple Time Capsule - Yes, still functioning well.
  • HomePod mini - My daily voice-activated radio.
  • Zeppelin Air - Probably the first AirPlay speaker. May replace it with HomePod eventually when the stars align.
  • iPad Pro 11-inch - My entertainment device.
  • Dyson Digital Slim - Yes, I do housework. It is lightweight, good for my tiny arm. Eyeing at Dyson Submarine now.

On the move

  • iPhone 14 Pro - Self-explanatory.
  • AirPod Pro 2 - Apple is good at creating product that you didn’t know you need it until you use it. This is one of those.
  • Apple Watch SE - I got it for free from a credit card promotion gift.

macOS apps

  • Visual Studio Code - Seems like the industry standard for code editor. Looks better with PyCharm Theme.
  • iA Writer - A minimal note app that can help me focus in writing. I bought it for iOS too.
  • Termius - A SSH platform to access servers.
  • TradingView - I use it to look at stock market charts, acting like a pro.
  • FreeForm - A whiteboarding app by Apple. I use it to draft flowchart for my work.
  • Hidden Bar - A utility app to hide cluttered icons in the top bar.
  • Magnet - Another utility app to quickly resize windows using shortcut keys.

iOS apps

I will only mention apps that are worthy. FYI, I use a lot of Apple native apps.

  • Spark - I've tried many mail apps and have given Apple Mail app plenty of chances, but I always ended up with Spark.
  • Calendar 5 - My requirement for calendar app is to be able to view events in monthly view. This app does it well.
  • Google News - I prefer Apple News app, but it is not available in Singapore. So this is the next best thing.
  • X - I mostly use it to get updates. I post when I'm in the mood.
  • LumiHealth - To get free money from government.
  • Spotify - As much as I want to be in Apple's ecosystem, this is still the winner.
  • Shortcuts - I created some shortcuts to make my life easier. I will share it someday.
