Sharing the hardware and software I use in my day-to-day life:

### Study room
- **MacBook Air M1** - *I'm happy with it, may consider the 15" model in the next upgrade.*
- **Apple Thunderbolt Display** - *Bought it cheap ($220) on Carousell. The best 2K display. Will change to the Apple Studio Display when I'm feeling rich.*
- **IKEA SIGFINN Monitor Stand** - *I needed my monitor to be higher than my MacBook Air, was using a stack of books previously.*
- **Razer Mouse & Mouse Pad** - *Don't ask me the model; I've been using it for years! All I know is the old reliable wired model.*
- **IKEA VADHOLMA Kitchen Island** - *Yeah, I use it as my work desk. A bit overkill if you ask me now. But I can sit or stand while working without any adjustment.*
- **IKEA FRANKLIN Bar Stool** - *Not the most comfortable, but it works.*

### Living room
- **Sony 40" LCD TV** - *This trusty TV doesn't give me an excuse to replace it!* 
- **Apple TV HD** - *To make my TV smarter.*
- **Apple Time Capsule** - *Yes, still functioning well.*
- **HomePod mini** - *My daily voice-activated radio.*
- **Zeppelin Air** - *Probably the first AirPlay speaker. May replace it with a HomePod eventually when the stars align.*
- **iPad Pro 11-inch** - *My entertainment device.*
- **Dyson Digital Slim** - *Yes, I do housework. It is lightweight, good for my tiny arm. Eyeing the Dyson Submarine now.*
- **SMATRUL Smart Plug** - *To control my home appliances and schedule them to turn on or off automatically.*

### On the move
- **iPhone 14 Pro** - Self-explanatory.
- **AirPods Pro 2** - *Apple is good at creating products that you didn't know you needed until you use them. This is one of those.*
- **Apple Watch SE** - *I got it for free from a credit card promotion gift.*

### macOS apps
- **Visual Studio Code** - *Seems like the industry standard for a code editor. Looks better with the PyCharm Theme.*
- **iA Writer** - *A minimal note app that can help me focus on writing. I bought it for iOS too.*
- **Termius** - *An SSH platform to access servers.*
- **TradingView** - *I use it to look at stock market charts, acting like a pro.*
- **FreeForm** - *A whiteboarding app by Apple. I use it to draft flowcharts for my work.*
- **Hidden Bar** - *A utility app to hide cluttered icons in the top bar.*
- **Magnet** - *Another utility app to quickly resize windows using shortcut keys.*

### iOS apps
- **Spark** - *I've tried many mail apps and have given the Apple Mail app plenty of chances, but I always end up with Spark.*
- **Calendar 5** - *My requirement for a calendar app is to be able to view events in a monthly view. This app does it well.*
- **Google News** - *I prefer the Apple News app, but it is not available in Singapore. So this is the next best thing.*
- **X** - *I mostly use it to get updates. I post when I'm in the mood.*
- **LumiHealth** - *To get free money from the government.*
- **Spotify** - *As much as I want to be in Apple's ecosystem, this is still the winner.*
- **Shortcuts** - *I created some shortcuts to make my life easier. I will share them someday.*

### Default app or service
Notes: Apple Notes
To-Do: Apple Reminders
Photo Management: Apple Photos
Photo Editing: Apple Photos (Vivid Cool 50)
Calendar: Calendars 5
Cloud File Storage: iCloud
Contacts: Apple Contacts
Browser: Safari
Chat: WhatsApp, iMessage & Telegram for closed group
Word Processing: Google Docs
Spreadsheets: Google Sheets
Presentations: Apple Keynote
